ABOUT

GraphicDen is a distinctive web tool directed towards creating a realtime graphical and visual representation of your data.
It does the tedious task of plotting graphs from complicated data for you , which is a long drawn out task if done manually.
The form of visual and graphical representation we offer includes bar graph,pie chart and line graph(more to come soon).
All you have to do is just upload the Excel spreadsheet format (.csv) and we will take care of the rest of it.
